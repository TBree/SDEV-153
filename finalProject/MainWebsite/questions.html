Questions And Answers:

Can I receive a refund for booking a party reservation or event?
- For your “Party Bookings” refunds can be given. The guest will need to request a cancelation within 24hrs of the booking

- For “Guest Events Booking” no refunds are avaliable. However, we will reschedule any booking request. Rescheduling will need to be changed within 1 weeks of the original schedule. For more information..... Call Us: 1-888-333-5555

Will food be available for purchase?
- Not at this location. We do accept most delivery services (uber eats, etc.); our fridge, freezer and heaters are available for use.

Can the guest touch the maze art?
- Yes, absolutely! There are different textures they can explore while entering different pathways.

What does the membership Include?
This is a monthly membership, which will include unlimited skate days with a 3 hour time limit. If you would like to sign up. Fill out the fom on the membership page.
